Design

"Does this hurt after four hours?"

Every Butter theme follows four principles from JetBrains' Darcula — the most-loved dark theme in professional IDEs.

Never pure black

Warm-tinted medium-dark backgrounds reduce eye strain. Pure black causes "halation" — text appears to glow on dark backgrounds.

Never pure white text

Cream/muted foreground at ~5.5:1 contrast. Passes WCAG AA without burning your eyes on late nights.

Functionally distinct syntax

Each token type gets its own color family. Your brain learns the pattern — reading speed increases over time.

Muted base, vivid accents

Desaturated backgrounds let amber syntax pop without neon glare. AMOLED and HC are intentional exceptions.

Ready to install?

Works on VS Code, Cursor, Kiro, VSCodium, Gitpod, Theia and any editor using the Open VSX Registry.


Search "Butter Bear" in your editor's Extensions panel, or run:

code --install-extension butterdev.butter-bear
